| stem:[a] a| half-width of a rectangular distribution of possible values of input quantity stem:[ii(X)_i]:

++++

++++

++++

a| lower bound, or lower limit, of the deviation of input quantity stem:[ii(X)_i] from its estimate stem:[x_i]:

[stem%unnumbered]
++++

++++

[stem%unnumbered]
++++
c_i -= del f//del x_i
++++

| stem:[f]
| functional relationship between measurand stem:[ii(Y)] and input quantities stem:[ii(X)_i] on which stem:[ii(Y)] depends, and between output estimate stem:[y] and input estimates stem:[x_i] on which stem:[y] depends


| stem:[del f//del x_i] a| partial derivative with respect to input quantity stem:[ii(X)_i] of functional relationship stem:[f] between measurand stem:[ii(Y)] and input quantities stem:[ii(X)_i] on which stem:[ii(Y)] depends, evaluated with estimates stem:[x_i] for the stem:[ii(X)_i]:

[stem%unnumbered]
++++
del f // del x_i = {: del f// del ii(X)_i \|_{x_1,x_2,...,x_N}
++++

| stem:[k] | coverage factor used to calculate expanded uncertainty stem:[ii(U) = ku_{"c"} (y)] of output estimate stem:[y] from its combined standard uncertainty stem:[u_{"c"}(y)], where stem:[ii(U)] defines an interval stem:[ii(Y) = y pm ii(U)] having a high level of confidence


| stem:[k_p] | coverage factor used to calculate expanded uncertainty stem:[ii(U)_p = k_p u_{"c"}(y)] of output estimate stem:[y] from its combined standard uncertainty stem:[u_{"c"}(y)], where stem:[ii(U)_p] defines an interval stem:[ii(Y) = y pm ii(U)_p] having a high, specified level of confidence stem:[p]


| stem:[n] | number of repeated observations

| stem:[ii(N)] | number of input quantities stem:[ii(X)_i] on which measurand stem:[ii(Y)] depends

| stem:[p]

[stem%unnumbered]
++++

++++

| stem:[q] | randomly varying quantity described by a probability distribution

.2+| stem:[bar(q)]

| estimate of the expectation or mean stem:[ii(mu)_q] of the probability distribution of stem:[q]


| stem:[q_k] | stem:[k]th independent repeated observation of randomly-varying quantity stem:[q]

| stem:[r(x_i,x_j)]
a| estimated correlation coefficient associated with input estimates stem:[x_i] and stem:[x_j] that estimate input quantities stem:[ii(X)_i] and stem:[ii(X)_j]:

[stem%unnumbered]
++++
r (x_i,x_j) = u (x_i,x_j)// [u(x_i) u(x_j)]
++++


| stem:[r(bar(ii(X))_i,bar(ii(X))_j)]
a| estimated correlation coefficient of input means stem:[bar(ii(X))_i] and stem:[bar(ii(X))_j], determined from stem:[n] independent
pairs of repeated simultaneous observations stem:[ii(X)_{i,k}] and stem:[ii(X)_{j,k}] of stem:[ii(X)_i] and stem:[ii(X)_j]:

[stem%unnumbered]
++++
r(bar(ii(X))_i,bar(ii(X))_j) = s(bar(ii(X))_i,bar(ii(X))_j)//[s(bar(ii(X))_i)s(bar(ii(X))_j)]
++++


| stem:[r(y_i,y_j)] | estimated correlation coefficient associated with output estimates stem:[y_i] and stem:[y_j] when two or more
measurands or output quantities are determined in the same measurement

| stem:[s_{"p"}^2] | combined or pooled estimate of variance

| stem:[s_{"p"}] | pooled experimental standard deviation, equal to the positive square root of stem:[s_{"p"}^2]


.3+| stem:[s^2(bar(q))]
| experimental variance of the mean stem:[q]
a| estimate of the variance stem:[ii(sigma)^2//n] of stem:[bar(q)]:

[stem%unnumbered]
++++
s^2 (bar(q)) = s^2 (q_k)//n
++++
| estimated variance obtained from a Type A evaluation


.3+| stem:[s(bar(q))]
| experimental standard deviation of the mean stem:[bar(q)], equal to the positive square root of stem:[s^2(bar(q))]
| biased estimator of stem:[ii(sigma)(bar(q))] (see <<note_C-2-21>>)
| standard uncertainty obtained from a Type A evaluation


.2+| stem:[s^2(q_k)]
| experimental variance determined from stem:[n] independent repeated observations stem:[q_k] of stem:[q]
| estimate of the variance stem:[ii(sigma)^2] of the probability distribution of stem:[q]


.2+| stem:[s(q_k)]
| experimental standard deviation, equal to the positive square root of stem:[s^2(q_k)]
| biased estimator of the standard deviation stem:[ii(sigma)] of the probability distribution of stem:[q]


.2+| stem:[s^2(bar(ii(X))_i)]
| experimental variance of input mean stem:[bar(ii(X))_i], determined from stem:[n] independent repeated observations stem:[ii(X)_{i,k}] of stem:[ii(X)_i]
| estimated variance obtained from a Type A evaluation


.2+| stem:[s(bar(ii(X))_i)]
| experimental standard deviation of input mean stem:[bar(ii(X))_i], equal to the positive square root of stem:[s^2(bar(ii(X))_i)]
| standard uncertainty obtained from a Type A evaluation

| estimate of the covariance of means stem:[bar(q)] and stem:[bar(r)] that estimate the expectations stem:[ii(mu)_q] and stem:[ii(mu)_r] of two

| estimated covariance obtained from a Type A evaluation


.2+| stem:[s(bar(ii(X))_i,bar(ii(X))_j)]
| estimate of the covariance of input means stem:[bar(ii(X))_i] and stem:[bar(ii(X))_j], determined from stem:[n] independent pairs of

|estimated covariance obtained from a Type A evaluation

| stem:[u^2(x_i)]
a| estimated variance associated with input estimate stem:[x_i] that estimates input quantity stem:[ii(X)_i]

NOTE: When stem:[x_i] is determined from the arithmetic mean or average of stem:[n] independent repeated
observations, stem:[u^2 (x_i) = s^2(bar(ii(X))_i)] is an estimated variance obtained from a Type A evaluation.


| stem:[u(x_i)]
a| standard uncertainty of input estimate stem:[x_i] that estimates input quantity stem:[ii(X)_i], equal to the positive
square root of stem:[u^2(x_i)]

NOTE: When stem:[x_i] is determined from the arithmetic mean or average of stem:[n] independent repeated
observations, stem:[u(x_i) = s(bar(ii(X))_i)] is a standard uncertainty obtained from a Type A evaluation.


| stem:[u(x_i,x_j)]
a| estimated covariance associated with two input estimates stem:[x_i] and stem:[x_j] that estimate input quantities stem:[ii(X)_i] and stem:[ii(X)_j]

NOTE: When stem:[x_i] and stem:[x_j] are determined from stem:[n] independent pairs of repeated simultaneous observations,
stem:[u(x_i,x_j) = s(bar(ii(X))_i,bar(ii(X))_j)] is an estimated covariance obtained from a Type A evaluation.


| stem:[u_{"c"}^2(y)] | combined variance associated with output estimate stem:[y]

| stem:[u_{"c"}(y)] | combined standard uncertainty of output estimate stem:[y], equal to the positive square root of stem:[u_{"c"}^2(y)]

| stem:[u_{"cA"}(y)] | combined standard uncertainty of output estimate stem:[y] determined from standard uncertainties and
estimated covariances obtained from Type A evaluations alone

| stem:[u_{"cB"}(y)] | combined standard uncertainty of output estimate stem:[y] determined from standard uncertainties and
estimated covariances obtained from Type B evaluations alone

| stem:[u_{"c"}(y_i)] | combined standard uncertainty of output estimate stem:[y_i] when two or more measurands or output
quantities are determined in the same measurement

| stem:[u_i^2(y)]
a| component of combined variance stem:[u_{"c"}^2(y)] associated with output estimate stem:[y] generated by
estimated variance stem:[u^2(x_i)] associated with input estimate stem:[x_i]:

[stem%unnumbered]
++++
u_i(y) -= [c_i u(x_i)]^2
++++

| stem:[u_i(y)]
a| component of combined standard uncertainty stem:[u_{"c"}(y)] of output estimate stem:[y] generated by the
standard uncertainty of input estimate stem:[x_i]:

[stem%unnumbered]
++++
u_i (y) -= abs(c_i) u(x_i)
++++


| stem:[u(y_i, y_j)] | estimated covariance associated with output estimates stem:[y_i] and stem:[y_j] determined in the same
measurement

| stem:[{u(x_i,x_j)}/{abs(x_i x_j)}] | estimated relative covariance associated with input estimates stem:[x_i] and stem:[x_j]

[stem%unnumbered]
++++

++++

++++
