== Terms and definitions

[.boilerplate]
=== {blank}

The terms and definitions of <<jcgm100>>, <<jcgm101>>, <<jcgm102>> and <<jcgm200>> apply.

ISO, IEC and IUPAC maintain terminological databases for use in standardization at the following addresses:

* IEC Electropedia: available at `http://www.electropedia.org`
* ISO Online Browsing Platform: available at `http://www.iso.org/obp`
* IUPAC Gold Book: available at `http://www.goldbook.iupac.org`
